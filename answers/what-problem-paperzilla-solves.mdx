Paperzilla solves a two-sided research problem:

- You do not want to miss important new papers.
- You also do not want to spend hours on weak matches.

It addresses this by combining project-level relevance, Must Read prioritization, and controlled delivery across feed, RSS/Atom, CLI, and email digests.
