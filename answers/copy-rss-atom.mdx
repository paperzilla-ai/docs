title: "How do I copy my RSS/Atom URL?"
description: "Use the Copy RSS/Atom URL action on your project page or use the CLI --atom flag."
---

<AiAgents path="/answers/copy-rss-atom" />

From the web app:

1. Open your project.
2. Click **Copy RSS/Atom URL**.
3. Paste into your feed reader.

From the CLI:

```bash
pz feed <project-id> --atom
```

If copy fails, verify you are signed in and retry.
