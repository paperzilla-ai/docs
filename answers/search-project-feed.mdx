1. Open a project from the [Dashboard](https://paperzilla.ai/dashboard).
2. Use the search box above the paper list.
3. Search by title, author name, or summary terms.

You can combine search with the feedback filters to focus on:

- **No feedback**
- **Useful**
- **Very useful**
- **Not for me**
